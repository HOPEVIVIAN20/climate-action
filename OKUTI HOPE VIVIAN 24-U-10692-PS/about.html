BACKGROUND INFORMATION

VISION STATEMENT

To create a thriving, environmentally conscious community in Uganda, where trees and green spaces flourish, and future generations can enjoy a healthy environment.

MISSION STATEMENT

"To inspire and empower communities to plant, nurture and protect trees, promoting a greener future for All.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Promote environmental sustainability through tree planting and conservation
  2. Educate communities on the importance of trees
  3. Empower local communities to take ownership on their environment
  4. Enhance biodiversity and mitigate climate change impacts

OUR SERVICES

REFORESTATION

Offer free and low-cost seedlings to encourage re_afforestation.

NATIVE PLANTING

Empower local communities to plant more trees and take ownership of their environment.

NURTURING

Regularly monitoring and caring for newly planted trees.
